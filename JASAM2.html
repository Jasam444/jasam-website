Branches of chemistry

There are two main branches of chemistry.

i) Pure chemistry.

This is a branch of chemistry that deals with the attempt to getting the better understanding of nature.This branch of chemistry does not involve practical use or application but it studies the basic theories and knowledge of chemistry.It also involves exploring the properties, structure and behaviour of matter at the molecular and atomic level, discovery of compounds and improving technologies to make life easier and better for us.There are three(3) maim branches of pure chemistry.

a) Physical

This branch of pure chemistry combines the principles from chemistry and physics to study the structure of matter and their physical properties.

b)Inorganic

It studies non-carbon base compounds, elements, properties and reaction. It excludes carbon compounds but includes trioxocarbonates, carbon(IV) oxide and carbon(II) oxide.

c) Organic

It studies carbon base compounds their properties and behaviour.It focuses on the stucture and reactions of organic compounds.

ii) Applied chemistry

It is a branch of chemistry that uses the knowledge of chemistry to produce goods and other services for mankind.This branch of chemistry focuses only on the practical application of chemistry in various fields like biochemistry, medicine, pharmacy etc.

Important Application of Chemistry in Our Everyday Life.

Agriculture

Chemistry has helped in the agricultural sector by the maximizing of crop yield and minimizing of cost products. It helps in agriculture through the development of fertilizers, chemical pesticides and many more.

Food and nutrition

Chemistry has improved food preservation.It helps us to understand and practice the composition of different ingrdients to create different variety of food and also use chemicals as preservatives to preserve the food for later.

Transportation

During fuel production, chemicals are put together to produce the fuel. These fuels are used to fill vehicles which are used to transport humans beings and loads from one place to the other.
